# **App Name**: Plate Ledger

## Core Features:

- Daily Revenue Entry: Record daily revenue totals, categorized by payment type (e.g., cash, online accounts) and link to specific sales accounts.
- Party & Transaction Tracking: Add individual dining parties or tables and log their specific transactions, including order details.
- Detailed Expense Tracking: Log all business expenses with relevant details, payment method, and associated dates.
- Multi-Account Management: Set up and manage multiple cash and online payment accounts for accurate fund segregation.
- Financial Reporting Tool: View chronological ledger of all revenues and expenses, clearly distinguishing between invoice dates (business doing date) and payment dates (transaction dates) to separate cashflow from profit and loss.
- Period Summaries: Generate daily, weekly, and monthly summaries of revenue, expenses, and net profit for quick overview.
- AI Expense Categorization Assistant: Utilize an AI tool to automatically suggest categories for recorded expenses based on their description.

## Style Guidelines:

- A light color scheme to promote clarity and readability, suitable for a professional financial tool.
- Primary color: Muted, earthy green (#6FB08F), representing growth, nature, and reliability.
- Background color: Very light, almost neutral green-grey (#ECF2EE), offering a clean and unobtrusive canvas.
- Accent color: A rich, darker olive green (#68A11A), used to highlight key actions and provide visual emphasis without being overly distracting.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), chosen for its modern, objective, and highly legible appearance across various data displays and text sizes, ensuring a professional feel.
- Utilize simple, clean, and intuitive vector icons that clearly represent financial actions and management concepts without clutter.
- Implement a clean, card-based layout for data display, providing clear separation between different sections like daily revenue, expenses, and reports for easy digestion of information.
